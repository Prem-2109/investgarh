Chhattisgarh: Ease of doing business puts the state on the sixth rank in the country

The state of Chhattisgarh has secured the sixth position in the country in ease of doing business. Despite the Corona crisis, the state of Chhattisgarh has managed to retain its 6th position from 2018. The ranking released is based on the implementation of the business plan, improvement and environment. Despite funding constrain due to the Centre’s reduction in capital expenditure (CapEx), Chhattisgarh has managed to be in the top ten list of states with more private investment in the manufacturing sector in the third quarter (Q3) of 2020-21. Chhattisgarh has received Rs 10,228 crore investment projects between October and December 2020 as per the project reports. This further indicates that the state government’s new industrial policies are investor-friendly.
