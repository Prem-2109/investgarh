Launch of Investgarh Chhattisgarh

MOU signed between Government of Chhattisgarh and WEEXPOINDIA - Investgarh Chhattisgarh officially announced
Honourable Chief Minister Shri Bhupesh Baghel Ji and Mr Vinoth Kumar, CEO of WEEXPOINDIA, signed the MOU on September 2nd at the Chief Minister’s office, flagging off “Investgarh Chhattisgarh”- the Global Investors meet in February 2022.
The Event also had other notable delegates such as Shri Anil Kumar Tuteja- Director of Industries, Principal State Secretary- Shri Manoj Kumar Pingua and Shri Kawasi Lakhma - Hon’ble Minister, Commerce and Industry. Global Industry Leaders and Investment partners from 34 countries across the world, joined the event both in-person and by virtual means.
The Honourable Chief Minister launched the logo and website of Invetsgarh Chhattisgarh, shared his vision on the initiative, the opportunities for investments, collaborations and partnerships with the government of Chhattisgarh.
